    一、序列图sequenceDiagram#

    1. 关系#

    1. 线方向：->>
    2. 线形态：-.->>

    2. 标记#

    1. 线标记：A-->B: note
    2. 激活标记：+/- activate/deactivate
    3. 注释标记：note right of A/over A:
    4. 题目标记：title
      sequenceDiagram
    title bad
    Alice->>+John: Hello John, how are you?
    loop Healthcheck
        John->>-John: Fight against hypochondria
    end
    Note right of John: Rational thoughts!
